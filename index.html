About Me

Although I obtained a Business Management degree, I lacked the passion to pursue a business career. Instead, I became interested in programming after reflecting on my high school IT classes and seeing the captivating programming projects my computer science friends had created. Although I previously thought I needed a 4-year computer science or STEM degree to work in tech, I discovered that many people without such degrees had landed software engineering jobs after completing coding bootcamps.
Therefore, I enrolled in an online full-stack bootcamp, which I recently completed. I thoroughly enjoyed the programming community and the "a-ha" moments that come with coding. I'm eager to secure my first programming job and continue to expand my skills.
